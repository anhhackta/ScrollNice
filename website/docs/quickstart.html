Quick Start (2 minutes)

1. Launch

Run ScrollNice.exe. A semi-transparent blue rectangle appears on screen.

2. Scroll

By default, Mode Click L‚Üë R‚Üì is active:

  • Left click inside the zone ‚Üí scroll up
  • Right click inside the zone ‚Üí scroll down

3. Move the Zone

Click and drag the zone to reposition it anywhere on screen.

4. Hotkeys

Hotkey Action
Ctrl+Alt+S Toggle ScrollNice on/off
Ctrl+Alt+E Toggle Edit Mode (resize zone)
Ctrl+Alt+W Toggle wheel blocking

5. Settings

Right-click the tray icon ‚Üí Settings to open the settings dialog. Here you can:

  • Change scroll mode
  • Adjust sensitivity (scroll amount per click)
  • Resize the zone
  • Enable/disable click sounds
  • Block the physical mouse wheel
  • Start with Windows

6. Lock Position

Once the zone is where you want it, open Settings ‚Üí check "Lock Position" to prevent accidental dragging.
