Scroll Modes

ScrollNice supports 5 scroll modes. Change modes via Settings dialog (Windows) or the popup/options page (Extension).

Mode 1: Click L‚Üë R‚Üì (Default)

The simplest mode. Uses mouse buttons to determine direction.

  • Left click ‚Üí scroll up
  • Right click ‚Üí scroll down

Best for: General use, most intuitive for right-handed users.

Mode 2: Click L‚Üì R‚Üë (Reversed)

Same as Mode 1 but reversed.

  • Left click ‚Üí scroll down
  • Right click ‚Üí scroll up

Mode 3: Split Left/Right

The zone is split into two vertical halves. A visual line divides them.

  • Click left half ‚Üí scroll up
  • Click right half ‚Üí scroll down

Best for: Users who prefer single-button clicking without right-click.

Mode 4: Split Top/Bottom

The zone is split into two horizontal halves. A visual line divides them.

  • Click top half ‚Üí scroll up
  • Click bottom half ‚Üí scroll down

Best for: Tall zones positioned on the side of the screen.

Mode 5: Continuous Hold

Hold the mouse button to scroll continuously. Speed increases the longer you hold.

  • Hold left click ‚Üí scroll up gradually (accelerating)
  • Hold right click ‚Üí scroll down gradually (accelerating)

Best for: Long documents, reading mode.

Sensitivity

The Scroll Amount setting controls how far each click scrolls (in pixels). Default is 300px. Increase for faster scrolling, decrease for finer control.
