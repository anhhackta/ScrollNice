Core Concept

ScrollNice creates a semi-transparent floating rectangle on your screen. When you click inside this "zone", it converts your clicks into scroll events. The zone is:

  • Always on top ‚Äî visible over all windows
  • Draggable ‚Äî move it anywhere on screen
  • Resizable ‚Äî adjust width and height
  • Lockable ‚Äî prevent accidental movement
